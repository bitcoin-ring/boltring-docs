    What is the BoltRing?

    The BoltRing is the first Bitcoin DIY contactless payment ring. It uses NFC technology and the open-source Bolt Card system to bring the tap-to-pay experience to Bitcoin Lightning. You can operate the BoltRing with your self-sovereign Bitcoin Lightning setup or use it with a commercial service provider.
